## Conditions

### Pushing

When you make a Strike or Ability check using Might, you may move the Target along an orthogonal Line horizontally a number of meters equal to your PV/SV. You may choose to move alongside the Target. Any affected Entities begin falling at the end of their turn. You take no damage from a fall from a number of meters equal to or less than your PV/SV.

### Launching

When you make a Strike or Ability check using Might, you may move the Target vertically a number of meters equal to your PV/SV. You may choose to move alongside the Target. Any creatures begin falling at the end of their turn. You take no damage from a fall from a number of meters equal to or less than your PV/SV.
