# Ancestry Creation

At character creation, you have **2** slots for your Ancestry Powers. Unlike your other Powers, they are innate features of your character. Most Ancestry Powers do not require a Power roll, but if needed, the Potency of your Ancestry features is equal to 1 + half your level.

Each slot may be spent on one Ancestry feature outlined in the Ancestry Creation section of the rules. Minor features cost 1 slot, while Major features take all 2 slots in character creation. You can freely define your cosmetic traits, so long as they do not create a mechanical advantage, at no cost.

By default, your basic attacks deal Crushing, Slashing, Piercing, or Spirit Damage (or Death if you are Unliving). You can change between these types at will as Melee or Ranged Strikes, but you cannot apply multiple Damage types at once.

## Slot categories

Each Ancestry feature should belong to one of these categories:

1. **Elemental Tag**
2. **Sense**
3. **Movement**
4. **Trait**
5. **Gifted or Skilled**

# Elemental Tags

Spend 1 Ancestry slot to gain one Elemental Tag from the Elemental Tags list. Your body is now a source for that Element. Your basic attack may deal the Damage Type granted by that Elemental Tag. If your Elemental Tag lists two Damage Types, choose one when you gain the Tag. You may spend 2 Ancestry Slots on the Elemental Tag instead to gain Versatile on your basic attacks with those Damage Types.

# Senses

**Default Senses:**
You begin with **Sight [Precise]** and **Hearing [Imprecise]**. You may swap Sight [Precise] for Hearing [Precise] and pick another Imprecise Sense, such as Smell or Tremorsense.

A **Precise Sense** can locate a creature accurately enough to target it normally. Unless a feature says otherwise, a Precise Sense has a Range equal to the size of the map in Tactical Mode, so long as you can draw a direct line from yourself to a Detected Target without obstructions. This is typically hand-waved in Narrative Mode or is comparable to human sight.

An **Imprecise Sense** can detect presence or direction, but not exact position. A creature you can only sense using an imprecise sense is considered Concealed by you. An Imprecise Sense has a Range equal to double your Perception in meters.

## Sense Slot

Spend 1 slot to do one of the following:

- Gain one new **Precise Sense**.
- Upgrade one existing **Imprecise Sense** to **Precise**.
- Upgrade your Sight to **Darkvision** or **Infravision**.

## Truesight

**Truesight [Imprecise] [Major]**
You gain an Imprecise Sense that can perceive invisible creatures, illusions, and spirits within 12 meters.

**Limited Truesight [Precise] [Minor]**
Choose one:
- See invisible creatures.
- See spirits and ghosts.
- See through illusions.
- See true forms.

You gain Truesight [Precise] for the Entities of the chosen category within 12 meters.

# Movement

By default you have a Land speed equal to double your Agility rating.

## Movement Slot

Spend 1 slot to gain one movement type while using the Move action:

- **Climb:** You can climb on sheer surfaces and hang off ceilings. You Move at half speed while moving in this way.
- **Swim:** You can swim at your normal Move speed instead of half and you can breathe underwater.
- **Burrow:** You can move through soft earth, sand, mud, snow, or similar loose material.
- **Glide:** When you fall, you may Move up to half the distance fallen. You take no fall damage while conscious and able to Move.

### Flight

**Flight:** You can fly at your normal Move speed. Choose one complication:

- **Elemental Flight:** You must be standing on Terrain with the chosen Elemental Tag in order to take off and fly.
- **Grounded Flight:** You become Falling if you do not end your turn on solid ground. You cannot use this Flight while Falling.
- **Hover:** You fly at half your Land speed.
- **Rocket Flight:** You cannot use Stealth while flying. When you take off, Flammable Terrain adjacent to you gains Ignite X equal to your Ancestry Power Potency.
- **Winged Flight:** You need empty spaces equal to twice your Size on each side of you in order to spread wings and fly.

# Physical Descriptors

## Short [Minor]

You count as one Size smaller when: squeezing through spaces, Hiding behind Cover, taking fall damage, and being carried or thrown.

## Tall [Minor]

You count as one Size larger for Wrestling, lifting, carrying, reaching, and resisting forced movement.

## Extra Hands [Minor]

You gain one additional usable Hand or grasping Appendage. Your Appendage may hold Items and Interact, but you do not gain extra actions or attacks.

## Multi-limbed [Minor]

You gain one additional set of Hands. Your Hands may hold Items, but you must take a Minor Action to Switch Arms in order to use the Item or Interact using them.

## Armoured Body [Minor]

Gain DR 1 against one Physical damage type: Crushing, Slashing, Piercing, or Ballistic.

## Hollow Body [Minor]

You may store small objects inside your body. You take +1 damage from Crushing and Pressure.

## Heavy [Minor]

You count as two Sizes larger for weight. This includes: Wrestle, being Lifted, swimming, and falling.

# Traits

## Gifted [Minor]

Gain **+1 PP** at character creation.

## Skilled [Minor]

Gain one Skill at Rank 2 and one Skill at Rank 1.

## Tough [Minor]

You gain +2 to your Health pool.

## Hearty [Minor]

You gain +2 to your Stamina pool.

## Artificial Body [Minor]

Choose one:

**Automaton:** You are Artificial and Living. You replace Biological with Artificial. You are Bloodless, Anaerobic, and Autotrophic, but you require fuel, maintenance, charging, or another upkeep method. Choose one body material: Metal, Wood, Stone, or Clay.

**Construct:** You are Artificial and Unliving. You replace Biological with Artificial. You are Bloodless, Anaerobic, and Autotrophic, but you require fuel, maintenance, charging, or another upkeep method. You cannot benefit from ordinary Heal actions or medicine. You may be Repaired using Science or Warfare. You cannot be resurrected. Choose one body material: Metal, Wood, Stone, or Clay.

## Slime [Major]
You gain the Acid Elemental Tag. Choose one ability from each group:
- DR 1 (take 1 less Damage from Crushing/Piercing) or **Sticky**
- Boneless or Organless or Brainless

If you have blood, your blood becomes **Acid blood**. When you receive Slashing damage, deal 1 Acid Splash damage to all adjacent Entities.

**Sticky:** An adjacent creature cannot Disengage unless it first succeeds on an Escape Artist roll with a Hit+. If the creature is Grabbed, it succeeds only on a Strong Hit. This does not apply while you or the Target is Wet.

## Material

- Metal: You gain the Metal Elemental Tag.
- Wood: You gain the Wood Elemental Tag.
- Stone: You gain the Earth Elemental Tag.
- Clay: You gain Malleable.
- Glass: You gain Vulnerability to Crushing damage. When you receive Crushing damage, deal 2 Slashing Splash Damage to all adjacent Entities.

## Definitions

### Biology

- Biological: Biological is the default body type. A character is Biological unless a rule replaces Biological with another trait.
- Artificial: You are immune to disease, Poison damage, and Death damage.

### Life

- Living: You are healed by Life and damaged by Death.
- Unliving: You are healed by Death and damaged by Life. You are immune to Spirit damage and possession.
- Object: You have Integrity instead of Health and Hardness instead of Armour. Your Hardness is DR to all damage you are not Vulnerable to equal to the Hardness of your primary Material.

### Body

- Amorphous: You are Organless, Boneless, and Brainless.
- Anaerobic: You are immune to Suffocation damage, inhalation effects, and do not need to breathe.
- Autotrophic: You do not need to eat or drink.
- Bloodless: You are immune to Bleed damage and effects that require Blood.
- Boneless: You count as Size 0 for squeezing and you are immune to the Broken Bones condition.
- Brainless: *You do not have a brain.* You are immune to Brain Drain and Psychic damage, but cannot use Smarts Actions. You may still think and act normally.
- Incorporeal: You have no solid body. You can move through Creatures and Objects, but not Terrain. You cannot carry Objects unless they are also Incorporeal. You cannot Grab, Restrain, or be Grabbed or Restrained by corporeal Creatures. You gain Resistance to all Physical damage and Vulnerability to Spirit Damage, even if you would be otherwise Immune.
- Organless: You have Anaerobic and Autotrophic. You are immune to Pressure damage and effects that require organs.

### Malleable

You can mould your body. As an Interact action, choose one shape: Default, Flat, Narrow, Long, or Crude. You remain in that shape until you use this trait again.

#### Default

You use your normal body shape.

#### Flat

You count as Size -1 for cover. You cannot use Climb, Fly, or Swim.

#### Narrow

You count as Size -1 for squeezing. You gain +1 to Escape Artist.

#### Long

You gain +1 m reach for Interact actions. You count as Size +1 against Grab and Restrain.

#### Esoteric

You may change your silhouette. You gain +1 to Perform checks to disguise your body shape. You do not change colour, scent, voice, clothing, or equipment.
