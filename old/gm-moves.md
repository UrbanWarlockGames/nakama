### GM Move: Complication
Cost: 1 Malice
Trigger: A Player rolls a Weak Hit.
Effect: The Player receives a Complication to their roll. The GM decides if the Player rolls or the GM chooses the Complication.
Mark XP or PP progress. Mark XP if the Complication comes from a tactical decision, skill check, social pressure, Drive, Strike, or personal failure. Mark PP if the Complication comes from using, resisting, overextending, losing control of, or being changed by a Power.
