## Actions
At the start of your turn, you have one **Basic Action** and one **Minor Action**. You may convert your Basic Action into two Minor Actions, so long as you have not used it yet. If you have two Minor Actions, you may convert them into one Basic Action.

Most Actions are a Basic Action unless specified. The generic Minor Actions are:
- Move
- Interact (with an Object, Item, or Terrain)
- Use a Skill
- Raise a Shield
- Ready a Reaction (any Basic or Minor Action + Trigger)
- Basic Attack (Strike! = Strike Value + Item Bonus)
