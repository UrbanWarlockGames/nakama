## Elemental Tags
Powers with the corresponding Elemental Tag deal the associated damage type and apply any Vulnerability Conditions on a Hit+ to Targets with the associated Vulnerability unless otherwise specified. Creatures with the Elemental Tag as an Ancestry feature also gain the corresponding Weaknesses, Vulnerabilities, and other traits specified. Creatures with an Elemental Tag may use one of their associated damage types for their basic attacks. You cannot have more than one Elemental Tag as an Ancestry feature.
- Acid: **Acid** damage. Applies **Corroded 1** on a Strong Hit. Immune to **Corroded**. Vulnerability to **Wet** damage.
- Ash: **Fire** damage + **Suffocating** (contact) + **Ignite 1** on Strong Hit. Vulnerability to **Wind** and **Wet** damage.
- Earth: **Crushing** damage. Applies **Slowed** on a Strong Hit. Resistance to **Fire**. Vulnerability to **Frost** and **Wet** damage.
- Electric: **Electric** damage. Applies **Charged 1** on a Strong Hit or on a Hit+ against **Wet** Targets. Immune to **Charged**. Vulnerability to **Poison** damage.
- Fire: **Fire** damage. Applies **Ignite 1** on a Strong Hit or Contact with **Flammable X**. Immune to **Ignite**. Vulnerability to **Wet** damage.
- Frost: **Frost** damage. Applies **Frozen 1** on a Strong Hit if the Target is Wet. Turns Wet Terrain into Slippery Terrain. Immune to **Frozen**. Vulnerability to **Crushing** and **Fire** damage.
- Gravity: **Crushing** and **Pressure** damage. On Hit+ you may Shift the Target SV/PV meters. An unwilling Target may resist with [Might] + [Wrestle] + [Size]. On a Strong Hit, the Target also becomes Prone. Vulnerability to **Wind** damage.
- Metal: **Piercing** or **Electric** damage. Heavy. Resistance to **Slashing** damage. Vulnerability to **Wet** and **Acid** damage.
- Poison: **Poison** damage. Applies **Poisoned 1** on a Strong Hit. Immune to **Poisoned**. Vulnerability to **Incapacitating** damage.
- Oil: **Poison** damage. Applies **Flammable 1** condition on Contact. Immune to **Poisoned**. Vulnerability to **Fire** and **Wet** damage.
- Smoke: **Suffocation** damage. Squares you enter become **Smokey** for a number of rounds equal to your Target Strike Value/Potency Value. If all squares in an enclosed space become **Smokey**, the duration becomes Permanent until a 1x1 meter opening is made in the room, after which the duration returns to normal. All creatures within Smokey terrain are Concealed unless they have smokesight or non-sight or smell precise senses. Smokey terrain makes creatures Hidden instead of Concealed to characters with infravision. Vulnerability to **Wind** damage.
- Vapour: **Wet** damage + **Suffocating** (contact). Makes Creatures and Objects in Area Wet but not Terrain or Self. Immune to **Flammable**. Resistance to **Fire** damage. Vulnerability to **Electric** and **Frost**, and **Wind** damage.
- Water: **Wet** damage. Applies **Wet 1** on Contact. Applies **Wet 1** to Self and squares you end your turn in. Immune to **Flammable**. Resistance to **Fire** damage. Vulnerability to **Electric** and **Frost** damage.
- Wind: **Wind** damage. Applies **Deafened 1** on Contact. Immune to **Deafened**. Vulnerability to **Wind** and **Pressure** damage.
- Wood: **Incapacitating** or **Poison** damage. Resistance to **Wind** damage. You are **Flammable 1**.
