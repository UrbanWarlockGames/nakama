# Power Index

## Hurl

The **Hurl** Ability moves the **Target** forcefully in an orthogonal line from their initial position to another position within 1 meter a number of meters determined by the Hit Tier. The damage dealt depends on whether the Target is **Physical** (Collision, representing moving a physical entity through space) or **Elemental** (Potency Value, representing your raw Power).

:::power title="Hurl" key="Attack, Damage, Forced Movement"
Move the Target forcefully in a Line. Physical Targets use Collision, Falling, or hazardous terrain for damage. Elemental Targets deal Potency damage.
:::

### Techniques

:::power title="Explode" key="Hurl, Technique, Damage, Area" cost="2"
Hurl deals Potency damage in a Medium Area on impact.
:::

:::power title="Mark" key="Hurl, Technique, Charm" cost="2" tier="—|MARKED|MARKED"
Apply the Marked condition to an Obstacle the Target you Hurl collides with, ending your Target's movement.
:::

:::power title="Ricochet" key="Hurl, Technique, Forced Movement" cost="3" tier="BASIC""
After impact, you may Move the Target again a number of meters equal to the Distance of the Tier Result. If the Target's path intersects with a Creature, Object, or Terrain, its movement stops immediately before Collision.
:::

:::power title="Launch" key="Hurl, Technique, Forced Movement" cost="3"
You may Move the Target vertically upward along its path. You may not move it downward. If the Target ends its movement off the ground, it is Falling until the end of its turn, when it falls.
:::

:::power title="Pin" key="Hurl, Technique, Charm" cost="4" tier="—|—|PRONE"
Knock the Target or Obstacle Prone.
:::

## Control

The **Control** Ability allows you to Interact with a General Physical Target. The Target may feel your touch. If the Ability is Elemental, they may feel the Element harmlessly, such as heat, cold, or static. An Unwilling Target can only be under the effect of one Control Technique at once.

:::power title="Control, Basic Duration" key="Control"
Interact with a General Physical Target. The Target may feel your touch. Elemental Control may carry a harmless sensory trace, such as cold for Frost Powers.
:::

### Techniques

:::power title="Communication" key="Control, Technique, Communication" cost="2"
Communicate with the Target. They hear your voice as a distant echo, but cannot directly place its source or point of origin unless they can Sense you using the Power.
:::

:::power title="Charm" key="Control, Technique, Charm" cost="3" tier="TRUST +1|TRUST +2 MINOR COMMAND|TRUST +3 COMMAND CHARMED"
Influence the Target's Disposition or issue a Command. Commands use the Target's listed action and must obey the limits of the Hit Tier.
:::

:::power title="Taboo" key="Control, Technique, Charm, Damage, Reaction, Basic Duration" cost="4" tier="HALF PV|BRAIN DRAIN|ACTION FAILS"
Declare one Taboo action the Target cannot break without suffering the consequences. If they attempt the Taboo action, make a Power Roll immediately as a free reaction. This lasts for a basic Duration or until they successfully perform the Taboo action.
:::

:::power title="Truth/Lies" key="Control, Technique, Communication, Charm" cost="4" tier="3 QUESTIONS|SIGNAL|FORBID"
Choose either Truth or Lies. You detect, signal, or forbid the chosen kind of answer according to the Hit Tier.
:::

:::power title="Confuse" key="Control, Technique, Charm" cost="4" tier="OBLIVIOUS|CONFUSED|BERSERK"
Disorient the Target. You may choose the effect of a lower Hit Tier if it is beneficial to you.
:::

:::power title="Puppet" key="Control, Technique, Charm" cost="6" tier="RESIST + E/T|E/T|NO RESIST"
Take direct control of the Target during its turn in Tactical Mode for a number of rounds equal to PV. In Narrative Mode, this lasts for 10 × the remaining Duration minutes. If the Target resists or the Effect ends, you take -2 ongoing to future Puppet attempts against it.
:::

:::power title="Mind Blank" key="Control, Technique, Memory" cost="6"
In Tactical Mode, the Target forgets the past number of rounds equal to the Duration of the Power. In Narrative Mode, it forgets the past 10 × the remaining Duration minutes.
:::
