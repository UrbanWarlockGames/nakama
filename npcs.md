Non-Player Character Card
 • Durability = [ Level + Tension ]
 • Injury Tracker = [ 5 + Level + Tension ]
 • Damage = 2d6 (weak), 3d6 (moderate) [choose which is Physical, which is Elemental]
 • Ultimate Attack: Roll a number of d6 equal to current marks on the Injury tracker once per Scene [choose damage type]
 Add +1d to all damage = 1/4 Party Level

Each time damage surpasses Durability, mark 1 Injury, clear Durability pool. Overflow remains in Durability pool. Each time the enemy gains an Injury, describe or ask the Player to describe how they inflict a serious Injury on them. Then, describe how they get back up.
When an enemy has 1/2 of their Injury tracker marked, they are Bloodied. When it is Bloodied, a NPC will choose to either Flee or Fight.
