## Story Structure

- Scene: When you switch from Narrative, Tactical, or Negotiation Mode, the Scene changes. There are usually 4-6 Scenes in an Episode. This could roughly equate to one "Encounter".
- Episode: There are usually 4-10 Episodes in an Arc. Downtime Mode counts as an Episode. This could roughly equate to a "Mission" or "Quest".
- Arc: There are usually 2-3 Arcs in a Season. You gain one Level at the end of an Arc. You gain one Level at the end of an Arc.
- Season: There are usually 3-6 Seasons in a Saga.
- Saga: The entire adventure from start to finish. At the end of a Saga, the group narrates the Epilogue in turn, describing what happens to their characters following the events of the Saga.
- Series: A Series may have an unlimited number of Sagas. Typically, recurring characters reset to Level 1 at the start of a new Saga, but it is more common to follow a new cast at the start of a new Saga.
